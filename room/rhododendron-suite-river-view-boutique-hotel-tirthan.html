Immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature with a stay in our Rhododendron Suite, a haven of eco-friendly opulence spanning 391 square feet. Crafted for the discerning traveler who values sustainability without sacrificing luxury, this suite comfortably houses up to three guests.

Sustainable Features:

  • Organic King-Size Bed: Rest in harmony with nature on our organic king-size bed, complete with natural fiber linens and a selection of organic pillows.
  • Eco-Chic Living Space: A spacious lounge area boasts furnishings made from reclaimed and responsibly sourced materials, offering a stylish yet sustainable space to relax.
  • Green Bathroom Essentials: Refresh in a bathroom outfitted with low-flow fixtures, solar-heated water, and a suite of organic bath products.
  • Nature-Forward Amenities: Enjoy the modern conveniences of LED lighting and energy-efficient appliances all designed to minimize our ecological footprint.
  • Expansive Natural Light Windows: Generous windows invite the outside in, offering breathtaking views of the enveloping foliage and flooding the space with natural light, while ensuring energy efficiency and insulation.

The Rhododendron Suite is more than just a place to stayit’s a commitment to eco-conscious living, enveloped in the lap of luxury.

Ausnya

Ausnya is a thoughtfully designed retreat nestled along the pristine riverbanks of Tirthan Valley, Himachal Pradesh minutes from the UNESCO Great Himalayan National Park. Our property provides direct access to premier Himalayan hiking trails and world-class trout fishing waters. Guests enjoy luxurious riverside suites with panoramic mountain views, where contemporary design complements the pristine natural surroundings. Perfect for adventure seekers, nature photographers and travelers seeking tranquility in the unspoiled Western Himalayas.
