Local Activities & Events

DIVE INTO THE RICH AND VIBRANT LIFE OF TIRTHAN VALLEY

Organic Bounty: Locally Sourced

Immerse yourself in the rich offerings of Tirthan Valley’s organic harvest. Indulge in a variety of locally grown fruits and vegetables, including apples, pears, figs, and peaches, straight from our own orchards. Embrace the flavor of nature’s bounty.

Wildlife Wonderland

Embark on an exploration of the Great Himalayan National Park, home to diverse wildlife such as snow leopards, brown bears, and musk deer. Let Tirthan Valley’s natural wonders captivate you on this adventurous journey.

Captivating Delights

Discover Tirthan Valley’s beauty and attractions. From Serolsar Lake and Chhoie Waterfall to Jalori Pass and Jibhi, be awed by nature’s wonders. Marvel at landmarks like the Budhi Nagin Temple and Raghupur Fort. Embrace an unforgettable journey through the captivating Tirthan Valley.

Ausnya

Ausnya is a thoughtfully designed retreat nestled along the pristine riverbanks of Tirthan Valley, Himachal Pradesh minutes from the UNESCO Great Himalayan National Park. Our property provides direct access to premier Himalayan hiking trails and world-class trout fishing waters. Guests enjoy luxurious riverside suites with panoramic mountain views, where contemporary design complements the pristine natural surroundings. Perfect for adventure seekers, nature photographers and travelers seeking tranquility in the unspoiled Western Himalayas.
